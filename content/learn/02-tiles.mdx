Now you can name any tile on the table. The three numbered suits — dots, bamboo, characters — form sequences and sets. Winds and dragons only stack into sets, never sequences.

That's the vocabulary. The next chapter shows you how these tiles combine into a winning hand — and what you're actually trying to build each round.
